Hello, I'm Ara

aspiring software engineer

Hi, I am

from Seoul, Korea. When I turned 15, my family moved to Hanoi, Vietnam where I spent my teenage years in an international school making friends from all over the world. After graduating high school, I came to London for my studies and I was immediately drawn to the city's diverse and lively culture. I have been working as a business manager in a global bank in the last 5 years.

About six months ago, I started coding in the evenings and weekends for fun. Programming has since become an important space for me to learn and be creative. I love that coding trains you to be both creative and logical.

When I'm not working or coding, I love cooking vegan food, practising yoga and running in the parks.

Why I want to be a programmer

Constant learning

I love that being a programmer entails constantly learning new technologies, codes and concepts. I have always loved learning and trying new things, and learning to code has been one of the most fun and rewarding experiences. I'm excited that I'll be exposed to constant learning and progress as a programmer.

Problem solving

I love the exhilarating moment when I finally figure out how to solve a problem I have been working on for a long time. The process of dividing big problems into smaller ones and solving them one by one gives me a sense of achievement. The process can be slow and frustrating but persistence and hard work often leads to a result.

Making an impact

What I truly love about programming is that it lets you build an idea into a reality. It is an empowering feeling to see a code changing something even if it is something small. I would love to build something that makes a positive impact in people's daily lives.
